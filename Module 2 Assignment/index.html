Panner

Panner is a also known as cottage cheese and is a lovely Indian dish most of the times used with a gravy and rotis. It can also be used with independently as a starter dish or used in Rice items like Panner rice etc. These items are available only during lunch and dinner time and takes some time to cook.

Dosa

Dosa is a breakfast food item that is made in South India ,it is crispy and is served with tangy chutney and spicy sambar and aloo bhaji and masala. Types of Dosa are Masala Dosa , Standard Dosa ,Set Dosa and Onion Dosa .These items are available only during the morning hours or breakfast time.

Uppit

Uppit is made from rava and is one of the tastiest food items consumed for breakfast in India , it has great taste and health benifits at the same time!. Special Uppit is also Available with the desired type of vegetables and these items are avaialable only during the breakfast time.
